🍽️ Famous Food in New Zealand

New Zealand cuisine is a flavorful fusion of Māori, British, and Pacific Island influences. Here are a few favorites loved by locals and tourists alike:

Hāngi

A traditional Māori method of cooking food underground with heated stones, creating a smoky and tender flavor.

Pavlova

A national dessert—crispy meringue outside, soft marshmallow inside, topped with fresh fruit and cream.

Feijoa

This tangy green fruit is a Kiwi favorite, eaten fresh or made into jams, smoothies, and desserts.
